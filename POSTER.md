## The problem

You buy pasta. You already have three bags of pasta. You run out of olive oil because you forgot to add it to the list. You pay £3.50 for tinned tomatoes at Sainsbury's when they're £1.80 at Lidl.

Sound familiar?

---

## What LarderLedger does

**🥕 Pantry**
One tap to mark something as in stock or out. At a glance, see exactly what your household has and what's running low.

**🍲 Meals**
Register the meals you actually cook — spaghetti bolognese, chicken curry, whatever. Link in the ingredients each one needs. See at a glance how many ingredients you already have for any meal.

**📅 Meal planning**
Pick a date for a meal. A calendar shows your whole week and month at a glance. When you plan a meal, tick which ingredients you need to buy — they go straight onto your shopping list, tagged with the meal name.

**🏪 Store prices**
Record what things cost at different shops. LarderLedger works out the price per 100g (or whatever unit you choose) so you can compare like for like across stores.

**🛒 Shopping list**
Auto-generated from whatever you're out of. Items are tagged with the meal they're for so you always know why something's on the list. Tick things off as you go round the shop.

**📷 Receipt scanning**
Point your camera at a receipt (or paste the text). AI reads it and pulls out every item and price, ready for you to match up and save. No manual data entry.

---

## Built for households

Sign in with your email. Your pantry, meals, and shopping list are private to your household. Everyone in the house sees the same data in real time.
